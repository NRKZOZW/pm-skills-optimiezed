# pm-journey-gtm

Go To Market (GTM) journey plugin: scale your product to market. Guides PMs through GTM strategy, cross-functional alignment, unit economics optimization, and scaling roadmap to drive sustainable business growth.

## Skills (3)

- **cross-functional-alignment** — Framework for aligning all departments for a successful market launch and ongoing scaling.
- **unit-economics** — Deep analysis framework for business KPIs and unit economics including CAC, LTV, payback period, and channel ROI.
- **scaling-roadmap** — Framework for creating a scaling-phase product roadmap across market, product, and revenue expansion dimensions.

## Commands (2)

- `/pm-journey-gtm:run-gtm` — Run a full Go To Market cycle — from GTM strategy through organizational alignment, launch execution, and business scaling.
- `/pm-journey-gtm:check-unit-economics` — Analyze business KPIs and unit economics — CAC, LTV, payback period, and channel ROI with optimization recommendations.

## Fit Journey Context

This is **Stage 5 (Final)** of the Fit Journey (CPF > PSF > SPF > PMF > GTM).

**Inputs**: PMF evidence, ICP definition, core value clarity, optimized pricing, growth levers (from PMF)
**Outputs**: GTM execution plan, organizational alignment, business KPI dashboard, scaling roadmap
**Previous Stage**: Run `/run-pmf` to measure and achieve product-market fit (pm-journey-pmf)
