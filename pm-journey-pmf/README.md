# pm-journey-pmf

Product Market Fit (PMF) journey plugin: measure and achieve product-market fit. Guides PMs through PMF measurement, growth hacking, core value deepening, and pricing optimization to reach self-sustaining growth.

## Skills (4)

- **pmf-measurement** — Comprehensive PMF measurement framework combining the Sean Ellis Test, retention curve analysis, NPS, engagement depth, and revenue signals into a unified PMF Scorecard.
- **pmf-survey** — Design, deploy, and analyze a PMF survey using the Sean Ellis Test and Superhuman PMF Engine with structured action frameworks.
- **growth-hacking** — Growth hacking playbook covering onboarding optimization, activation improvement, viral loops, retention, reactivation, and ICE-scored experiment prioritization.
- **core-value-deepening** — Framework for identifying and strengthening a product's core value through feature audits, opportunity scoring, and pricing alignment.

## Commands (2)

- `/pm-journey-pmf:run-pmf` — Run a full Product Market Fit cycle — measure PMF, design growth experiments, deepen core value, and optimize for self-sustaining growth.
- `/pm-journey-pmf:measure-pmf` — Quick PMF health check — score your product against the PMF Scorecard with improvement recommendations.

## Fit Journey Context

This is **Stage 4** of the Fit Journey (CPF → PSF → SPF → PMF → GTM).

**Inputs**: Released MVP, KPI baselines, initial user feedback, retention data (from SPF)
**Outputs**: PMF evidence, ICP definition, core value clarity, optimized pricing, growth levers
**Previous Stage**: Run `/run-spf` to build and validate your MVP (pm-journey-spf)
**Next Stage**: Run `/run-gtm` to bring your product to market at scale (pm-journey-gtm)
